BIOGRAPHY

Eniola Korty Olanrewaju Popular known by her social name korty eo is a 24 years old filmmaker, YouTuber, graphic designer, model, and artist.

korty eo was born on April 2, 1998, in Lagos, Nigeria, she studied Computer Science at the University of Ibadan. she later proceeded to upgrade her skills in video production and editing.








CAREER AND EARLY LIFE

Korty started modeling in 2017 after signing with Few Models. She walked the runway for Lagos Fashion and Design Week and GTCO Fashion Weekend and modeled for Lisa Folawiyo’s SS18 Kwenu collection.

she studied Computer Science at University but Later went on to learn video production and editing which got her first Job at Zikoko Mag. she spent over a year producing shows before she became the Head of Content at Empawa Africa.

she worked at Empawa for a year, In March 2018, Korty started working as a video producer for Zikoko before working for Mr. Eazi’s emPawa Africa as Head of Content in January 2020. She quit in December 2020 to focus on her YouTube channel.

Korty joined YouTube on 18 January 2019 and uploaded her first video in July 2020. As of November 2021, her channel has gained over 80,000 subscribers. She began her video series “Flow, With Korty” in November 2020 and has since interviewed various celebrities like Don Jazzy, Ayra Starr, Rema, and Blaqbonez.
